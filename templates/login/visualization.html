About Word Clouds

Word clouds show the most important words in your journal entries, with larger words meaning they appear more often or are more important. This helps you spot common themes and ideas in your writing. You can create word clouds that focus on specific feelings, such as positive, neutral, or negative moods, or you can filter them by a time range, like the past month or year. This gives you a better understanding of how your thoughts and feelings change over time.
